Thank you for choosing Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP for your software and IT solutions. We strive to ensure our customers are satisfied with our services, including our "Frazor Open Connect" platform. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP may provide a refund, the process for claiming a refund, and the responsibilities of the customer.

1. General Policy

Our refund policy is designed to be transparent and fair. Refunds are not guaranteed and are subject to the terms outlined below. Each refund request will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

For subscription-based services, terms of cancellation and potential refunds will typically be governed by the specific subscription agreement entered into at the time of purchase.

For project-based services or custom development, refund terms will be detailed in the specific project proposal or contract agreed upon before the commencement of work.

2. Eligibility for a Refund

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances (subject to review and approval by Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP):

  • Service Non-Delivery: If we fail to deliver the agreed-upon service or product within the stipulated timeframe and this failure is solely attributable to Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP.
  • Major Defects: If the software or service provided has critical defects that render it unusable, and we are unable to rectify these defects within a reasonable period after being notified. The definition of "critical defect" and "reasonable period" will be determined by Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP in consultation with the client.
  • Cancellation of Subscription (if applicable): For monthly or annual subscription services, you may be eligible for a partial refund if you cancel your subscription in accordance with the terms of your subscription agreement. Typically, refunds are not provided for the current billing cycle but future charges may be stopped. [Frazor to specify actual subscription refund terms.]
  • Billing Errors: If you have been incorrectly billed due to an error on our part, we will issue a refund for the overcharged amount.

3. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will generally not be provided in the following situations:

  • You have changed your mind after purchasing the service or software.
  • The service has been substantially delivered or utilized.
  • Issues arising from your own infrastructure, third-party integrations not managed by us, or your misuse of the service.
  • Minor bugs or issues that do not significantly impede the core functionality of the service, and for which we are actively working on a resolution.
  • Failure to provide necessary information or cooperation from your side, leading to delays or issues in service delivery.
  • Services explicitly marked as "non-refundable" at the time of purchase.
  • Fees for consultation, setup, or customization work that has already been performed.
  • If you have violated our Terms and Conditions.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must submit a written request to our support team. Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your full name and company name.
  • Contact information (email and phone number).
  • Service or product for which you are requesting a refund.
  • Date of purchase.
  • Invoice number or transaction ID.
  • A detailed explanation of the reason for your refund request, including any supporting documentation (e.g., screenshots, error logs).

Refund requests should be sent to: support@frazor.com (or another designated email by Frazor).

5. Refund Processing

Once we receive your refund request, we will review it and notify you of our decision within a reasonable timeframe (e.g., 10-15 business days).

If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment, within a certain number of days (e.g., 7-10 business days after approval), depending on your card issuer's policies or bank processing times.

We reserve the right to offer partial refunds or credits for future services in certain situations.

6. Policy Modifications

Frazor Enterprise Solutions LLP reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our Services after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Refund Policy.
